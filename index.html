Quantitative Finance + ML/DL Engineering Roadmap

From Python Basics to AI-Driven Quant Research

A structured roadmap for building quantitative trading systems, financial ML pipelines, backtesting infrastructure, and deep learning research environments from scratch.

Primary Goal

Become capable of building and validating quantitative research systems instead of blindly training prediction models.

Best Starter Market

Crypto markets provide free APIs, strong volatility, easier experimentation, and more inefficiencies than large-cap equities.

Core Philosophy

Infrastructure quality, validation rigor, and feature engineering are more important than model complexity.

Important Principles

Feature engineering matters more than fancy models.

Backtesting correctness is critical.

Most market data is noisy and non-stationary.

Avoid data leakage at all costs.

55% prediction accuracy can already be exceptional.

Markets are often better at predicting volatility than direction.

Research discipline beats hype.
